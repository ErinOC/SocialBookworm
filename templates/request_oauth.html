Being a bookworm can get lonely.

The Social Bookworm suggests author appearances, book signings, and book clubs to attend with friends.

How it Works:

1. The worms burrow through your friends’ Goodreads shelves, finding the authors that your friends have read or wish to read.

2. If there are events related to these authors in your area*, the worms will let you know.

3. If you’re also interested in an event, you can get the party started!

*The Social Bookworm currently works only for events in the United States.

Get Started:

Authorize SocialBookworm to access your Goodreads data.

SocialBookworm does not store any of your personal Goodreads data.
