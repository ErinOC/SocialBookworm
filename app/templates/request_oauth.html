Being a bookworm can get lonely.

SocialBookworm suggests author appearances and book clubs to attend with friends.

The worms burrow through your friends’ Goodreads shelves, finding the authors that your friends have read or wish to read. If these authors have events in your area*, the worms will let you know. If you’re also interested in an event, you can get the party started!

Authorize SocialBookworm to access your Goodreads data.

*SocialBookworm currently works only for events in the United States.

SocialBookworm does not store any of your personal Goodreads data.

Powered by (but unffiliated with) Goodreads.
